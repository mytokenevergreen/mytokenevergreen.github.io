About

EVERGREEN

EVERGREEN is a token on Binance Smart Chain boasting a number of impressive features. It is a token with a Black Hole design that exponentially cuts the total supply in circulation by massive amounts. EVERGREEN combines this with an innovative Auto-Liquidity feature that increases liquidity of the token rapidly. And finally, EVERGREEN has massive decentralization on a scale rarely seen in other tokens. Combine these three together and you get a power house token out of the hands of anyone, except the community as a whole.

BLOCK HOLE

ALGORITHM

The Black Hole owned over 50% of supply at launch. The Black Hole counts as one of the wallet holders that 2% of transactions are distributed to. This results in exponential growth of the black hole and exponential burn of the token supply. At the time of this writing, the black hole has already grown to 55% percent ownership of the total supply.

Unruggable

Liquidity is locked in VipSwap and ownership of the contract has been transferred to the burn address.

Fair Launch

100% of the EVERGREEN supply was seeded as liquidity. That means no presale and no allocation to team members.

Safe Auto-farming

2% of all transactions are distributed to holders. Watch your wallet climb the minute you begin holding.

100% Decentralized

EVERGREEN token is community led and managed by volunteers, since ownership has been burned and launch was completely seeded as liquidity.

Continuously Rising Price Floor

3% of every transaction is locked as liquidity in the VipSwap EVERGREEN/BNB pool, creating an ever rising price floor.

Whale Shock Safety

No wallet holds more than 1% of token supply and no transaction can involve more than 5 trillion tokens.

NFT FARM

EVERGREEN Token has an innovative NFT Farm where you can stake EVERGREEN tokens or partner tokens to earn beautiful NFTs! When you stake your tokens, you earn points which can be used to select the NFTs you’d like. There will be many more NFTs uploaded over time, including tons from our partners! So it’s a good idea to start staking your tokens right away and start earning points. As we form new partnerships, you will also have the ability to earn NFTs from different Binance Smart Chain coins you might hold. Our Piggy Bank is stuffed with benefits for you to enjoy!
