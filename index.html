ASKED QUESTIONS

  • How do I buy EVERGREEN?

    EVERGREEN is only available on Binance Smart Chain. To use Binance Smart Chain, you must configure your wallet to use the Binance Smart Chain network. Then you would need to own BNB tokens for gas fees. Finally you would need to swap for EVERGREEN Token on an exchange like VipSwap.

  • Why do I get an error when I try to buy or sell on VipSwap?

    During times of high trade volume, prices will fluctuate greatly and may lead to an error. To account for this, you must increase the slippage.
